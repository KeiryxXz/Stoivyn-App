About Stoivyn

Stoivyn is a Stoic habit tracking and personal development app designed to help you cultivate the four cardinal virtues: Wisdom, Courage, Justice, and Temperance.

Key Features:

  • Daily habit tracking and ritual management
  • Progress visualization and streak monitoring
  • Personal reflection journal
  • Virtue assessment and growth tracking
  • Achievement system and milestone celebrations
  • Stoic wisdom integration

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using Stoivyn?

Download the app, create an account, and begin with the welcome assessment to set up your first ritual and track your progress.

What are the subscription options?

Stoivyn offers monthly and 3-month subscription plans that unlock premium features and advanced tracking capabilities.

How do I track my daily habits?

Set up your rituals in the app, and use the daily tracking feature to mark your progress and maintain streaks.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can request a copy of your data by contacting our support team.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings or the App Store subscription management page.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

App won't load or crashes

Try restarting the app, updating to the latest version, or reinstalling if the issue persists.

Can't access premium features

Ensure your subscription is active and try restoring purchases in the app settings.

Data not syncing

Check your internet connection and try logging out and back in to refresh your account.
